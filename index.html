How AI Coach Works

1

Game Data Tracking

Listens to live match events via Overwolf Game Events SDK without violating Riot's ToS.

2

AI Analysis

Processes game events and generates contextual tactical callouts using OpenAI's GPT models.

3

Voice Generation

Converts AI text into realistic Valorant agent voices using ElevenLabs advanced text-to-speech.

4

Team Communication

Plays voice through virtual mic so your teammates hear the tactical callouts during gameplay.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this allowed by Riot Games?

The application is designed to be fully compliant with Riot's Terms of Service. We only use the Overwolf SDK, which is an approved third-party tool for Valorant. We're currently in the approval process with Riot.

How do teammates hear the AI coach?

The app uses a virtual audio cable (like VB-Cable) to play the AI voice through your microphone, allowing teammates to hear the callouts as if they were coming from you.

What agent voices are available?

Currently, we have AI voices for Brimstone, Cypher, and Jett. More agent voices will be added based on user feedback and demand.
